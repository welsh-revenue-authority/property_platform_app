Features

If you are designing and building public services you can use the land and property platform to use property and land information within your product or service.

You can also use the platform for analysis and research purposes with many data sets being freely available to download.

The land and property platform makes it easy to:

  • Use land and property information in your product or service
  • Download datasets for offline analysis and research

API integration

You can integrate the Land and property API with your web application or back-office system to use land and property information in your service.

Get started with the API.

Analysis and research

You can use the platform to download data sets for further analysis and research helping you reach trusted conclusions.

Learn more about the available data.
