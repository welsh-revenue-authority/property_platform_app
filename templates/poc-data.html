Proof of concept

Data used in this proof of concept

To make this proof of concept as realistic as possible, without using data about real properties and taxes, we created synthetic data and scenarios.

This platform only contains data about three fictional places: Cwmderi, Pontypandy and Upper Leadworth, which we have located on military or ex-military land. We have used real address identies (UPRNs, postcodes, etc), but created fake addresses.

We have also created two hypothetical tax zones: north east and south west.

Areas covered by fictional places

The 'town' of Cwmderi is located on the land of MOD Castlemartin. It is fully in the south east tax zone. The UPRNs of all properties in Cwmderi are listed in MOD_Castlemartin__Cwmderi_UPRN.geojson. The 'town boundaries' and the UPRNs of all properties are available as geojson.

The 'town' of Pontypandy is located on the land of ex-RAF Llanbedr. It is split across the north east and south west tax zones. The 'town boundaries' and the UPRNs of all properties are available as geojson.

The 'town' of Upper Leadworth is located on the land of MOD Sealand. It is fully in the North tax zone and shares a border with England. The 'town boundaries' and the UPRNs of all properties are available as geojson.

To illustrate certain functions, the platform also has a small about of data about properties outside of Wales. The UPRNs of these are also available as geojson.

Fictional tax zones

The boundaries of the two tax zones are arbitrary, although they do follow ward boundaries. The boundaries of the north east zone and the south west zone are available as geojson.
